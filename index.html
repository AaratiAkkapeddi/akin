A·kin is a project by Aarati Akkapeddi in collaboration with stars.archive and commissioned by The Photographers’ Gallery exploring human and non-human categorisation of photographic archives.

The work highlights issues in grasping anthropological, historical and cultural notions about personal and collective identity when photographs are considered as mere data points.
