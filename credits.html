A·kin is a project using photography, video, machine vision and website development by Aarati Akkapeddi in collaboration with stars.archive, commissioned by The Photographers’ Gallery.

We invite you to get in touch to share additional information, send any questions or request the removal of images of loved ones.

Neuronal Network Model: VGG-16 by Karen Simonyan and Andrew Zisserman at the University of Oxford.

Risograph Printing: Earthbound Press
